# Feature Video — Shot-by-Shot Template

Reusable template for a single feature video. Total length: 45–75s. Format: 1080×1920 (Reels).

## Script slots

Replace bracketed text per feature.

| Time     | Layout                     | Voiceover                                                  | On-screen                                            |
|----------|----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| 0:00–0:02 | Zoomed-in screen           | (silent, sound effect only)                                | The end-result moment — "Booked ✓" toast, count tick |
| 0:02–0:07 | Screen + camera bubble     | "When a client [problem situation]…"                       | Dashboard idle state                                 |
| 0:07–0:50 | Screen only, auto-zoom on  | "[Feature name] [does X]. Here's how it works."            | Walkthrough — 3 click moments max                    |
| 0:50–0:55 | Screen + camera bubble     | "And you didn't lift a finger."                            | Pull-back to full dashboard                          |
| 0:55–1:00 | Camera only / outro card   | "StyleDesk.ai. Link in bio."                               | Logo + URL                                           |

## Hook bank (first 2 seconds)

Pick one — the hook decides whether the Reel gets watched:

- "Your phone rings at 2am. StyleDesk picked up."
- "She texted at 11pm. She booked at 11:01."
- "47 missed calls. 47 booked clients."
- "Watch what happens when nobody answers your phone."
- "The text your front desk would have missed."

## Caption template

Word-by-word highlight, lower third. Mirror the voiceover exactly — don't paraphrase.

## Music cues

- 0:00 — drop on the hook frame
- 0:07 — drop volume by 6 dB when voiceover starts
- 0:50 — swell back up for the payoff
- 0:55 — outro hit

## Posting checklist

- [ ] Exported 1080×1920 MP4 from Descript
- [ ] First frame is the hook (not a logo, not a face)
- [ ] Captions burned in (not relying on Instagram's auto-captions)
- [ ] Cover image set in Instagram (use a frame from 0:50–0:55)
- [ ] Caption text under the post: hook + 1 line + CTA + 3–5 hashtags
