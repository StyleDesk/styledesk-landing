# Instagram — Format Specs

Use these exact dimensions when exporting from Descript.

## Reels (primary format)

- Aspect ratio: **9:16**
- Resolution: **1080×1920**
- Length: 15–90s (sweet spot: 30–60s)
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Codec: H.264, MP4
- Max file size: 4 GB

## Feed video post

- Aspect ratio: **4:5** (vertical) or **1:1** (square)
- Resolution: 1080×1350 (4:5) or 1080×1080 (1:1)
- Length: up to 60s for in-feed playback
- Cover image: pick a clean frame, not mid-blink

## Stories

- Aspect ratio: **9:16**
- Resolution: 1080×1920
- Length: 15s per card (longer videos auto-split)
- Keep critical content inside the **safe zone**: 250px from top, 250px from bottom — Instagram UI overlays the edges

## Safe zones for Reels

Don't put text or logos in these areas — Instagram covers them with UI:

- Top: 220px (handle + sound)
- Bottom: 400px (caption + buttons)
- Right: 180px (like / comment / share rail)

Center your captions in the middle 60% of the frame.

## Export settings (Descript)

- Resolution: 1080p
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Bitrate: High (or 10 Mbps custom)
- Audio: AAC, 256 kbps, stereo
- Format: MP4
