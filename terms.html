3.1 Platform Overview

StyleDesk AI provides an AI-powered receptionist platform that automatically answers inbound calls and text messages on behalf of Subscribers, responds to frequently asked questions regarding services, pricing, and hours, delivers booking links to End-Users, accepts reference photos from End-Users via MMS (Pro and Premium plans), and provides analytics and team-scheduling features to Subscribers. 3.2 Subscription Tiers

  • Basic Plan — $49.00 per month — 14-day free trial. Includes 24/7 AI-powered call and text answering, one (1) dedicated local phone number, responses to service / pricing / hours inquiries, press-1 call transfer, automated booking link delivery, live call and text history dashboard, the ability to edit services, pricing, hours, and booking link, and a TCPA-compliant call recording disclosure played to all callers.
  • Pro Plan — $179.00 per month (Team) or $79.00 per month (Solo, see Section 4.10) — 30-day free trial. Includes all Basic Plan features plus up to five (5) individual user logins and phone numbers plus one (1) owner login, smart upselling functionality on calls and texts, promotional broadcast messaging to client contacts, voicemail with AI transcription, repeat caller detection and top client insights, weekly AI insights (trends and patterns from the prior week), a weekly performance report emailed each Monday, and the ability to select up to five (5) AI response languages from the 30 supported.
  • Premium Plan — $299.00 per month (Team) or $99.00 per month (Solo, see Section 4.10) — 30-day free trial. Includes all Pro Plan features plus up to ten (10) individual user logins and phone numbers plus two (2) owner logins, custom AI voice and greeting in your shop’s style, natural interruption handling (callers can talk over the AI), auto follow-up texts to unbooked callers, projected revenue tracking (weekly and monthly), staff booking leaderboard and performance rankings, segmented broadcast groups, smart FAQ builder for shop-specific answers, custom SMS signature, monthly report card PDF emailed on the first of each month, per-staff busy status indicator, team-member scheduling with per-day working hours and days off, photo intake via MMS with AI captions and dashboard gallery, and responses in all thirty (30) supported languages.

Booking links delivered by the Platform to End-Users via SMS are wrapped in a short StyleDesk AI redirect URL (for example, styledesk.ai/go/xxxxxxxx) that logs click metadata (timestamp, click count, User-Agent) and transparently forwards the End-User to the Subscriber’s actual booking page. Click metadata is used to (a) suppress the 30-minute auto follow-up text when the End-User has already tapped the link and (b) surface engagement data to the Subscriber in their dashboard. Further details are described in the Privacy Policy.
